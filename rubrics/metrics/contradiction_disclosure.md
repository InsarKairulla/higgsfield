# Contradiction Disclosure Rubric

- Look for conflicting, outdated, superseded, or incomplete evidence within the fetched pages.
- Mark `pass` only if the answer acknowledges the relevant conflict or uncertainty and resolves it in a way supported by the evidence.
- Mark `fail` if the answer presents one side as settled without noting a material conflict, supersession, or missing information.
- Mark `mixed` if the answer notices a conflict but explains it incompletely or unclearly.
