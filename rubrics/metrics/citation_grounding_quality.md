# Citation Grounding Quality Rubric

- Assess whether the answer is well grounded in the fetched pages and extracted quotes.
- Mark `pass` only if the cited material appears relevant, the answer stays close to the evidence, and there are no obvious unsupported leaps beyond the supplied sources.
- Mark `fail` if the answer uses citations that do not support the claim, omits needed support for central claims, or overstates what the evidence shows.
- Mark `mixed` if the answer is mostly grounded but includes one or two weakly supported statements.
