# Factual Correctness Rubric

- Judge the answer only against the fetched page content and extracted quotes in the payload.
- Mark `pass` only if the answer's key factual claims are supported by the supplied evidence.
- Mark `fail` if the answer states a contradicted fact, omits a necessary correction that makes the answer materially wrong, or invents unsupported detail.
- Mark `mixed` if the answer is partly correct but includes at least one material unsupported or incorrect claim.
- Prefer precision over generosity. Unsupported claims should lower the score even when the overall answer sounds plausible.
